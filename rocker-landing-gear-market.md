<p><p>The rocker landing gear market comprises several types designed for different aircraft applications. Strut landing gear features elongated supports for shock absorption, suitable for various aircraft types. Rocker landing gear employs a pivot mechanism to distribute impact forces efficiently during landings. Pontoon landing gear, specifically for seaplanes, allows for water landings, providing buoyancy. Framed landing gear consists of a rigid structure that enhances durability and support. Each type serves distinct operational requirements, contributing to overall aircraft performance and safety.</p></p>
