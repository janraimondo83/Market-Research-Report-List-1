<p><ul><li>Traction</li><li>Hydraulic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The machine room-less elevator market comprises two main types: traction and hydraulic elevators. Traction elevators utilize a system of ropes and pulleys, providing energy efficiency and smooth operation, ideal for mid to high-rise buildings. Conversely, hydraulic elevators use a piston-driven system, offering strong lifting capacity at lower heights, making them suitable for low-rise buildings. Both systems eliminate the need for a traditional machine room, optimizing space and reducing construction costs, enhancing their appeal in modern architecture.</p></p>
