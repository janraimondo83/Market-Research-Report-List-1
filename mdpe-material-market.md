<p><p>The MDPE (Medium-Density Polyethylene) material market encompasses various production methods, primarily the autoclave and tubular processes. The autoclave process involves using high-pressure conditions to polymerize ethylene, resulting in a more uniform product with tailored properties. In contrast, the tubular process entails continuous polymerization in a vertical reactor, allowing for higher production efficiency and lower operating costs. Both methods cater to diverse applications, influencing the mechanical, thermal, and chemical resistance of MDPE materials in various industries.</p></p>
