<p><p>Static relays are essential devices used in electrical systems for protection and control. The market can be categorized into three types based on output: AC output, DC output, and AC/DC output. AC output relays are designed for alternating current applications, often used in power distribution systems. DC output relays cater to direct current applications, essential in battery systems. AC/DC output relays provide versatility, enabling operations in both alternating and direct current environments, suitable for mixed or hybrid systems.</p></p>
