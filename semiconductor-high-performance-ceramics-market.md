<p><p>The semiconductor high-performance ceramics market includes specialized materials crucial for electronic applications. Semiconductor aluminas (Al2O3) ceramics are known for their electrical insulation and thermal conductivity. Aluminum nitride (AlN) ceramics offer excellent thermal management and high dielectric strength, making them suitable for high-power devices. Silicon carbide (SiC) ceramics provide superior thermal stability and efficiency, ideal for high-voltage applications. Silicon nitride (Si3N4) ceramics are recognized for their mechanical strength and thermal shock resistance, making them valuable in advanced semiconductor technologies.</p></p>
