<p><p>The polishing and lapping film market encompasses various types of films used to achieve surface finishing and smoothing in manufacturing processes. Stretch film is flexible and provides cling for load stabilization. Metallized film reflects light and offers barrier properties, useful in packaging. Adhesive film bonds surfaces together while offering a smooth finish. Shrink film shrinks tightly around objects when heated, providing protection and a cosmetic appeal. Each type serves distinct applications in industries requiring precise surface treatment and protection.</p></p>
