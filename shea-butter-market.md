<p><p>The shea butter market is categorized into two primary types: raw and unrefined shea butter, and refined shea butter. Raw and unrefined shea butter retains its natural vitamins and nutrients, making it ideal for cosmetic and medicinal uses. It is minimally processed, preserving its properties.
