<p><p>The gears gear reducers market encompasses three primary types: bevel gear reducers, worm gear reducers, and helical gear reducers. Bevel gear reducers utilize conical gears to transmit power between non-parallel shafts, suitable for right-angle configurations. Worm gear reducers consist of a worm and worm gear providing high torque reduction in compact spaces, ideal for heavy machinery. Helical gear reducers employ angled teeth for smooth and efficient power transmission, offering high-load capacity and better efficiency, commonly used in various industrial applications.</p></p>
