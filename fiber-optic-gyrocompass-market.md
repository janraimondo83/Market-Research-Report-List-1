<p><p>The Fiber Optic Gyrocompass market is segmented into two main types: Single-axis and Multi-axis Fiber Optic Gyrocompasses. Single-axis Fiber Optic Gyrocompasses are designed to measure rotation around a single axis, making them suitable for applications requiring simple navigation solutions. In contrast, Multi-axis Fiber Optic Gyrocompasses can detect rotation around multiple axes simultaneously, providing more comprehensive orientation and navigation data. This makes them ideal for complex applications in aviation, maritime, and other fields requiring precise three-dimensional positioning.</p></p>
