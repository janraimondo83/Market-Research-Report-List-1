<p><p>Redox flow cells are a type of rechargeable battery technology that stores energy in liquid electrolytes, allowing for flexibility in energy storage and discharge applications. They operate by circulating two electrolyte solutions through a reactor, where redox reactions occur, enabling energy conversion.
