<p><ul><li>Offset Gear Reduction Starter</li><li>Planetary Gear Reduction Starter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gear reduction starter market primarily features two types: offset gear reduction starters and planetary gear reduction starters. Offset gear reduction starters utilize a simple gear train design, allowing for efficient torque multiplication with minimal space. They are commonly used in various automotive applications. In contrast, planetary gear reduction starters offer a more compact design with multiple gear sets, providing higher torque output in a smaller package. This technology is favored in high-performance and heavy-duty vehicles due to its durability and efficiency.</p></p>
