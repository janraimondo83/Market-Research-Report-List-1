<p><p>The hydraulic hose and couplings market features two primary types: spiral wire and wire braided hoses. Spiral wire hoses are constructed with multiple layers of high-tensile wire wound in a spiral pattern, providing strength and flexibility for high-pressure applications. In contrast, wire braided hoses feature woven wire layers, offering good flexibility and durability but typically withstand lower pressures. Both types cater to various industries, ensuring efficient fluid transfer in hydraulic systems, with specific advantages depending on operational requirements.</p></p>
