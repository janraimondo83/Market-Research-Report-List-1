<p><ul><li>Vertical Platform Lifts</li><li>Incline Platform Lifts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wheelchair lifting platform market primarily includes two types: vertical platform lifts and incline platform lifts. Vertical platform lifts are designed to raise users vertically between different levels, offering a safe and accessible means of overcoming stairs or barriers. In contrast, incline platform lifts are structured to move along a sloped track, providing a smooth transition for wheelchair users on stairways. Both types enhance mobility and accessibility, catering to various environments such as homes, public buildings, and commercial spaces.</p></p>
