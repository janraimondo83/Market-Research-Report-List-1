<p><p>The residential ventilation systems market comprises various types designed to enhance indoor air quality. Exhaust ventilation systems remove stale air, creating a negative pressure that draws fresh air in. Supply ventilation systems introduce outside air to pressurize the home, improving airflow. Balanced ventilation systems combine both exhaust and supply, ensuring equal air exchange for optimal efficiency. Energy recovery systems transfer heat and humidity between incoming and outgoing air, improving energy efficiency while maintaining comfort levels in homes.</p></p>
