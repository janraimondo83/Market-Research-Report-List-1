<p><p>The Medical Equipment Maintenance Service market encompasses three main types: preventive, corrective, and operational maintenance. Preventive maintenance focuses on routine inspections and servicing to prevent equipment failures and ensure optimal performance. Corrective maintenance addresses issues after equipment malfunctions, involving repairs or replacements to restore functionality. Operational maintenance encompasses daily activities that ensure equipment runs effectively, including cleaning and minor adjustments. Together, these services enhance equipment reliability, prolong lifespan, and enhance patient safety in healthcare environments.</p></p>
