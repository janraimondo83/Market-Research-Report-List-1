Pneumatic pumps utilize compressed air for operation, making them suitable for high-speed applications with a low risk of hydraulic contamination. Hydraulic pumps rely on fluid pressure and are known for their power and efficiency, particularly in heavy-duty tasks. Electric pumps are driven by motors, offering precise control and low maintenance. Each type serves specific needs in construction and paving projects.</p></p>
