<p><p>The knee support market is primarily segmented into rigid and soft supports. Rigid supports, typically made from hard materials, provide maximum stability and immobilization, making them suitable for post-injury recovery or surgical support. They restrict movement to promote healing. Conversely, soft supports, often composed of elastic or neoprene materials, offer moderate compression and flexibility. They are designed for mild support during activities, helping to alleviate pain and reduce swelling while allowing a wider range of motion.</p></p>
