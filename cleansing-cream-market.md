<p><p>The cleansing cream market can be categorized into three main types: W/O (Water-in-Oil), O/W (Oil-in-Water), and Other formulations. W/O creams provide a thicker, more moisturizing texture, making them suitable for dry skin, as they create a barrier that locks in moisture. O/W creams, on the other hand, are lighter and absorb quickly, appealing to oily or combination skin types. The "Other" category encompasses specialty formulations like micellar water and clay-based cleansers, catering to diverse consumer preferences and specific skin concerns.</p></p>
