In contrast, synthetic lubricants are engineered for superior performance, offering enhanced thermal stability, reduced friction, and longer service life. They are ideal for high-performance gears and extreme conditions, leading to improved efficiency and reduced maintenance costs in various industrial settings.</p></p>
