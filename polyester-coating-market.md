<p><ul><li>Water-based Polyester Coating</li><li>Solvent-based Polyester Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyester coating market is primarily divided into two types: water-based and solvent-based formulations. Water-based polyester coatings utilize water as the primary solvent, offering lower levels of volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and promoting environmental sustainability. In contrast, solvent-based polyester coatings rely on organic solvents, providing excellent durability and adhesion properties, making them suitable for a wide range of applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, construction, and furniture, ensuring protection and aesthetic appeal to various surfaces.</p></p>
