<p><ul><li>Clamp Ammeter</li><li>Probe Ammeter</li><li>Fork Ammeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The current test device market includes various types of ammeters tailored for specific applications. Clamp ammeters measure current without direct contact, allowing for safe and efficient testing in live circuits. Probe ammeters utilize test probes to directly connect to the circuit, offering precise measurements but requiring disconnection of live wires. Fork ammeters, designed with a split design, can clamp around conductors like a clamp meter while providing more portability and versatility. Each type serves unique needs in electrical diagnostics and maintenance.</p></p>
