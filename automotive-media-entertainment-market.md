<p><p>The **OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer)** market refers to automotive media and entertainment systems installed by vehicle manufacturers during production. These systems are integrated seamlessly into the vehicle's design, offering high-quality, brand-specific features. The **aftermarket** market involves media and entertainment systems purchased separately and installed post-production. These systems provide customization options, upgrades, and compatibility with various vehicle models, catering to consumer preferences for enhanced functionality, modern technology, or cost-effective alternatives beyond factory-installed options. Both markets serve distinct consumer needs within automotive entertainment.</p></p>
