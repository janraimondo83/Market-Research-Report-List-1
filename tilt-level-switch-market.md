<p><p>The tilt level switch market is categorized into three main types: tilt float switches, mechanical tilt level switches, and others. Tilt float switches operate based on the buoyancy principle, activating at specific liquid levels. Mechanical tilt level switches utilize a hinged mechanism that tilts when a predetermined angle is reached, triggering a signal. Other types may include electronic or capacitive switches, which employ different sensing technologies for liquid level detection. Each type offers distinct advantages suited to various industrial applications.</p></p>
