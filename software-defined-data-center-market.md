<p><p>A Software-Defined Data Center (SDDC) is an advanced data center model that enables the abstraction and virtualization of all IT resources, including compute, storage, networking, and security. SDDCs leverage software to manage and provision resources dynamically, minimizing reliance on physical hardware.</p><p>The Software-Defined Data Center Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.4% during the forecast period. Additionally, the integration of artificial intelligence and machine learning within SDDC frameworks enables improved decision-making and resource management. Security and compliance concerns are also leading to innovations in SDDC offerings.
