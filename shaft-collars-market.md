<p><strong>The Shaft Collars Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Clamping Shaft Collars</li><li>Set Screw Shaft Collars</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shaft collars market comprises various types, primarily clamping shaft collars and set screw shaft collars. Clamping shaft collars utilize a radial clamping mechanism to securely hold components without marring the shaft, providing easy adjustability and removal. In contrast, set screw shaft collars feature screws that grip the shaft at specific points, offering a more permanent attachment but potentially damaging the shaft surface. Both types serve essential functions in automotive, industrial, and manufacturing applications by ensuring proper alignment and positioning of rotating elements.</p></p>
