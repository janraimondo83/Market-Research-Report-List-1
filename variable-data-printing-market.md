<p><p>The Variable Data Printing (VDP) market comprises two primary types: release liner and linerless printing. Release liner involves a backing material that separates printed labels from their adhesive surface, making them easy to handle and apply. In contrast, linerless printing eliminates this backing, reducing waste and allowing for continuous roll usage. Both types cater to personalized marketing and product labeling needs, enhancing efficiency and customization while addressing environmental concerns through sustainable printing solutions.</p></p>
