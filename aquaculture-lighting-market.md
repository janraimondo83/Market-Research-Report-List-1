<p><p>The aquaculture lighting market comprises primarily two types: halogen lighting and LED lighting. Halogen lighting, known for its bright output and warmth, can enhance the growth of aquatic organisms but tends to consume more energy and generate more heat, which can affect water temperature. LED lighting, on the other hand, is energy-efficient, long-lasting, and customizable in terms of spectrum, making it ideal for various aquaculture settings. Its lower heat production helps maintain optimal water conditions, contributing to healthier aquatic ecosystems.</p></p>
