<p><strong>The Injectable Hyaluronic Acid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-phase Product</li><li>Duplex Product</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Injectable Hyaluronic Acid market is divided into two primary types: single-phase products and duplex products. Single-phase products consist of a homogeneous gel, providing uniform viscosity and smooth injection, making them suitable for fine lines and subtle enhancements. In contrast, duplex products feature a dual-phase structure, combining a softer gel for surface application and a firmer gel for deeper tissue filling. This bimodal approach allows for greater versatility in aesthetic procedures, catering to varying patient needs and desired outcomes.</p></p>
