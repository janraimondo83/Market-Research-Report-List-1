<p><strong>The Linen Dryer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fluidized Bed Dryers</li><li>Conduction Dryers</li><li>Convection Dryers</li><li>Rotary Dryers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The linen dryer market comprises various dryer types tailored for efficient drying. Fluidized bed dryers utilize a stream of air to suspend and circulate linen, promoting even drying. Conduction dryers rely on direct contact with heated surfaces to transfer heat, ensuring rapid moisture removal. Convection dryers utilize hot air circulation to dry linen uniformly. Rotary dryers combine rotation with heat to achieve consistent drying by tumbling the linens, enhancing airflow and moisture removal. Each type offers unique advantages based on operational needs.</p></p>
