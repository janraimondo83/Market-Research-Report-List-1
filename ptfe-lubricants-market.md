<p><p>The PTFE lubricants market consists of three main types: dry film spray, fluid grease, and lubricant oil. Dry film spray provides a thin coating that reduces friction on surfaces and is ideal for high-temperature and high-load applications. Fluid grease offers excellent adhesion and is used in machinery for long-lasting lubrication, suitable for various industrial applications. Lubricant oil is versatile, providing smooth operation and reduced wear in engines and moving parts, making it essential for various automotive and industrial uses.</p></p>
