<p><p>The Automotive Blind Spot Detection System market includes various sensor types: Radar Sensors use radio waves to detect objects, offering long-range and weather-resistant capabilities. Ultrasonic Sensors rely on sound waves for short-range detection, ideal for close-proximity monitoring. LIDAR Sensors use laser pulses for high-precision, long-range object detection, excelling in detailed environmental mapping. Other technologies may include camera-based systems or hybrid solutions combining multiple sensors. Each type caters to specific needs, balancing range, accuracy, and cost, enhancing vehicle safety by alerting drivers to unseen hazards in blind spots.</p></p>
