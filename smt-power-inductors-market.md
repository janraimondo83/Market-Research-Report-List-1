<p><ul><li>Shielded</li><li>Unshielded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SMT power inductors market is categorized into two main types: shielded and unshielded inductors. Shielded inductors are designed with a protective casing that minimizes electromagnetic interference, making them ideal for high-frequency applications requiring reduced noise and better performance. In contrast, unshielded inductors lack this casing, offering a more compact design with lower cost but increased susceptibility to interference. Each type serves specific applications, with shielded inductors typically used in sensitive environments and unshielded inductors favored in cost-sensitive scenarios.</p></p>
