<p><ul><li>Hot Wire Airflow Sensor</li><li>Vane Airflow Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mass air flow sensor market includes several types, with hot wire air flow sensors and vane air flow sensors being prominent. Hot wire sensors measure the cooling effect of air on a heated wire, providing precise airflow readings, widely used in modern vehicles for optimal engine performance. Vane sensors employ a rotating vane that measures airflow based on its movement, commonly found in older automotive applications. Both types serve critical roles in fuel management and emissions control for internal combustion engines.</p></p>
