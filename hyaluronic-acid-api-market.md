<p><p>The Hyaluronic Acid API market comprises two primary types: animal tissue and microbial fermentation. Animal tissue-derived hyaluronic acid is sourced from animal cartilage, offering high molecular weight and biocompatibility, commonly used in pharmaceuticals and cosmetics. In contrast, microbial fermentation involves producing hyaluronic acid through bacterial fermentation, providing a sustainable and ethical alternative, free from animal-derived components. This method allows for controlled production with consistent quality, catering to a growing demand for vegan and sustainable solutions in various applications.</p></p>
