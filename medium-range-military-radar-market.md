<p><p>The medium-range military radar market consists of several key components including antennas, transmitters, receivers, duplexers, displays, digital signal processors, and stabilization systems. Antennas capture and emit radar signals, while transmitters generate these signals. Receivers process incoming signals to extract useful information, and duplexers manage signal direction. Displays present radar data visually, aiding in decision-making. Digital signal processors enhance signal manipulation and analysis, and stabilization systems ensure accurate tracking despite movement or environmental factors, all critical for effective military operations.</p></p>
