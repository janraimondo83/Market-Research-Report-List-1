<p><p>The child-resistant packaging market comprises three main types: reclosable packaging, non-reclosable packaging, and special blister packaging. Reclosable packaging allows users to securely seal products after initial opening, ensuring ongoing safety. Non-reclosable packaging is designed for single-use, providing a permanent barrier to access. Special blister packaging features individual compartments for items, which are usually difficult for children to open while still being user-friendly for adults. Together, these categories enhance product safety and control access to harmful substances.</p></p>
