<p><ul><li>Floating</li><li>Roll</li><li>Arm Style</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial chain tensioner market can be categorized into three main types: floating, roll, and arm style. Floating tensioners adjust independently to changes in chain length, ensuring consistent tension without rigid mounting. Roll tensioners utilize a rotating drum to maintain pressure on the chain, enhancing longevity and performance. Arm style tensioners are fixed in position but feature a pivoting arm that applies tension, allowing for simple installation and effective chain management. Each type serves specific applications in various industries.</p></p>
