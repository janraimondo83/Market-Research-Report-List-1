<p><p>An Energy Management System (EMS) in the industrial market encompasses various technologies and platforms that optimize energy use. SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition) oversees real-time data; PLC (Programmable Logic Controller) automates processes; DCS (Distributed Control System) manages complex operations. Energy platforms aggregate data across systems, while energy analytics provide insights for decision-making. Meter Data Management (MDM) handles consumption data; EMIS (Energy Management Information Systems) streamline reporting. PLCS (Power Line Carrier Systems) enhance communication, and DRMS (Demand Response Management Systems) optimize energy demand.</p></p>
