<p><p>The indoor humidifier market features several types designed to improve air moisture levels. Ultrasonic humidifiers use high-frequency vibrations to produce a fine mist. Warm-mist humidifiers boil water to create steam, while evaporative humidifiers use a fan to blow air through a wet wick, promoting natural evaporation. Vaporizers heat water to produce steam, and steam-to-steam humidifiers generate steam from water and then condense it into a warm mist. Each type caters to different humidity needs and preferences.</p></p>
