<p><p>The LDPE film market is segmented primarily into General LDPE Film and Metalized LDPE Film. General LDPE Film is widely used for packaging due to its flexibility, low-density properties, and resistance to moisture, making it ideal for grocery bags, wraps, and containers. In contrast, Metalized LDPE Film has a thin layer of metal applied to enhance barrier properties, offering improved protection against light, moisture, and gases. This makes it suitable for applications requiring superior shelf life, such as food packaging and electronics.</p></p>
