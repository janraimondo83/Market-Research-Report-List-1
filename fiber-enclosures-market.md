<p><p>The fiber enclosures market consists of two primary types: wall-mount and rack-mount enclosures. Wall-mount enclosures are designed for space-constrained environments, allowing for easy installation on vertical surfaces, ideal for smaller networks or remote locations. In contrast, rack-mount enclosures fit into standard server racks, providing scalability and organization for larger network infrastructures. Both types facilitate efficient fiber management, protect sensitive connections, and enhance overall network performance, catering to various deployment needs across industries.</p></p>
