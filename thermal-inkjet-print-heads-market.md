<p><strong>The Thermal Inkjet Print-heads Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Inkjet</li><li>Random Inkjet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal inkjet print-heads market comprises two main types: continuous inkjet (CIJ) and random inkjet. Continuous inkjet technology operates by continuously circulating ink and ejecting droplets, suitable for high-speed applications like industrial printing. In contrast, random inkjet, also known as drop-on-demand, selectively fires ink droplets only when needed, making it ideal for consumer and office printers. Both types serve distinct sectors, with CIJ focusing on efficiency and volume, while random inkjet emphasizes precision and versatility in print quality.</p></p>
