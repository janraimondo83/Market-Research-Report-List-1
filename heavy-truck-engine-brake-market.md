<p><p>The Heavy Truck Engine Brake market includes two primary types: Compression Release Brakes and Exhaust Brakes. Compression Release Brakes work by releasing compressed air in the engine cylinders during the exhaust stroke, creating resistance to slow the vehicle. Exhaust Brakes restrict exhaust flow, increasing backpressure in the engine, which reduces speed. Both systems enhance braking efficiency, reduce wear on traditional brakes, and improve safety, especially on steep descents. Compression Release Brakes are more powerful, while Exhaust Brakes are simpler and cost-effective.</p></p>
