<p><p>The Sunroof Glazing market is divided into three main types: Single Glazing, Double Glazing, and Triple Low-E Glazing. Single Glazing consists of a single layer of glass, offering basic insulation and visibility. Double Glazing features two glass panes with an air gap, enhancing thermal insulation and reducing noise. Triple Low-E Glazing includes three glass layers with low-emissivity coatings, maximizing energy efficiency by reflecting heat while allowing natural light, ideal for climate control and noise reduction in vehicles.</p></p>
