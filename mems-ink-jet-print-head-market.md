<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The MEMS Ink Jet Print Head Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Print Head</li><li>Piezo Print Head</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MEMS inkjet print head market is primarily divided into two types: thermal print heads and piezoelectric print heads. Thermal print heads use heat to vaporize ink, creating bubbles that propel droplets onto the substrate, offering high-speed printing and simplicity in design. In contrast, piezoelectric print heads utilize mechanical pressure generated by piezoelectric elements to eject ink, providing precise droplet control and accommodating a wider range of ink types. Both technologies are integral to various printing applications, including consumer and industrial markets.</p></p>
