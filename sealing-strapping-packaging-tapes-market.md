<p><p>The sealing strapping packaging tapes market comprises three primary types: rubber-based, acrylic, and hot melt adhesive tapes. Rubber-based tapes offer strong adhesion and are ideal for heavy-duty applications, providing immediate bond strength. Acrylic tapes feature a water-based adhesive, offering UV resistance and long-term durability, suitable for various environments. Hot melt adhesive tapes provide a quick bonding solution, known for their versatility across different surfaces, making them popular in manufacturing and logistics for efficient packaging and strapping solutions.</p></p>
