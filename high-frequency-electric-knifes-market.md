<p><strong>The High Frequency Electric Knifes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monopolar Circuit</li><li>Bipolar Circuit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-frequency electric knives market is primarily categorized into monopolar and bipolar circuits. Monopolar circuit devices use a single electrode to cut tissue while grounding through a pad placed on the patient, allowing for deeper tissue penetration. In contrast, bipolar circuit devices utilize two electrodes to pass current directly through the tissue, minimizing surrounding thermal damage and providing more precision. Both types are essential in surgical settings, catering to varying needs for cutting and coagulation during procedures.</p></p>
