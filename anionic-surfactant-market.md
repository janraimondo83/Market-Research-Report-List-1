Secondary Alkane Sulfonate (SAS) is known for its surface-active properties. Methyl Ester Sulfonates (MES) are derived from renewable sources, while sulfosuccinates are used in mild formulations. Other types include specialty surfactants catering to niche applications.</p></p>
