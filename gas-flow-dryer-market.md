<p><p>The gas flow dryer market is categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I dryers utilize direct gas flow for efficient drying, making them suitable for applications requiring rapid moisture removal. In contrast, Type II dryers incorporate indirect heat transfer, providing more controlled drying conditions and minimizing the risk of product damage. Both types are essential in various industries, such as food processing and pharmaceuticals, addressing specific drying needs based on material characteristics and production requirements.</p></p>
