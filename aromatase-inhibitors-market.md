<p><strong>The Aromatase Inhibitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Selective Inhibitors"</li><li>"Non-Selective Inhibitors"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aromatase inhibitors market comprises two primary types: selective and non-selective inhibitors. Selective inhibitors, such as anastrozole and letrozole, target the aromatase enzyme specifically, effectively reducing estrogen production in breast cancer treatment with minimal side effects. Non-selective inhibitors, like formestane, affect both aromatase and other steroidogenic enzymes, leading to a broader reduction in estrogen levels but potentially more side effects. The differentiation influences prescribing practices and patient outcomes, shaping market dynamics and therapeutic strategies in oncology.</p></p>
