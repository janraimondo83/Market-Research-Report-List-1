<p><p>The Cafe POS system market is primarily divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based systems are hosted online, allowing cafes to access their POS software from anywhere with internet connectivity, providing scalability and automatic updates. In contrast, on-premises systems are installed locally on the cafe's hardware, offering greater control and security but requiring more maintenance. Each type caters to different business needs, with cloud solutions being favored for flexibility, while on-premises caters to those valuing data security and stability.</p></p>
