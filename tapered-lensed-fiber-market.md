<p><p>The tapered lensed fiber market is divided into two main types: single-mode fiber and multimode fiber. Single-mode fiber offers a smaller core, allowing only one light mode to propagate, resulting in lower attenuation and higher bandwidth over long distances. In contrast, multimode fiber features a larger core, enabling multiple light modes, which increases the data-carrying capacity over shorter distances. Both types cater to varying applications in telecommunications, data centers, and industrial settings, influencing market demand and technological advancements.</p></p>
