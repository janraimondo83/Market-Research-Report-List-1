Unsaturated ketones have at least one double or triple bond, offering higher reactivity and versatility, making them valuable in organic synthesis, specialty chemicals, and advanced materials. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications, with demand driven by their unique properties and functional benefits.</p></p>
