<p><ul><li>Internal Elevator Button Sterilizer</li><li>External Elevator Button Sterilizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Elevator Button Sterilizer market comprises two primary types: internal and external sterilizers. Internal elevator button sterilizers are integrated within the elevator system, automatically disinfecting buttons as users engage with them, enhancing hygiene without extra manual intervention. In contrast, external elevator button sterilizers are standalone devices, typically placed near the elevator, enabling users to sanitize buttons before or after use. Both types aim to improve cleanliness and reduce the spread of pathogens in high-traffic environments.</p></p>
