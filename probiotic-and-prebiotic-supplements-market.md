<p><p>Probiotic and prebiotic supplements are designed to enhance gut health by providing beneficial microorganisms (probiotics) and promoting the growth of these microorganisms (prebiotics). Probiotics are typically live bacteria, while prebiotics consist of non-digestible fibers that feed these bacteria, fostering a balanced gut microbiome. The increasing awareness of gut health's impact on overall wellness, digestive disorders, and immune function has driven the demand for these supplements.</p><p>The Probiotic and Prebiotic Supplements Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.5% during the forecast period. Additionally, advancements in product formulations and delivery methods, along with increased availability of options in both retail and online channels, are contributing to market expansion. The trend toward plant-based and organic formulations is also gaining traction as consumers become more health-conscious. Moreover, the integration of probiotics and prebiotics into various food and beverage products, alongside supplements, is further diversifying the market landscape, catering to a broader range of consumer preferences and needs.</p></p>
