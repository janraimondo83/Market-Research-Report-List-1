<p><ul><li>Chemistry Method</li><li>Microscopy Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automated urine analyzers are categorized into two primary market types: chemistry methods and microscopy methods. Chemistry method analyzers employ chemical reactions to determine the presence of various substances, such as proteins, glucose, and pH levels, providing a rapid and quantitative assessment of urine samples. In contrast, microscopy method analyzers utilize imaging technology to examine urine sediments, identifying cells, crystals, and pathogens. Both methods enhance diagnostic accuracy, streamline laboratory workflows, and contribute to efficient patient management in clinical settings.</p></p>
